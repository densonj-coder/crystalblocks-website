Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the Shop in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore any previously purchased gem packs, Crystal Pass subscription, or Remove Ads purchase linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my Crystal Pass subscription?

Open your iPhone's Settings > tap your name at the top > Subscriptions > Crystal Blocks > Cancel Subscription. You will retain Crystal Pass benefits until the end of your current billing period.

I lost my progress. Can it be recovered?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you signed in with Game Center, your leaderboard scores and achievements are preserved. However, gem balance, cosmetic unlocks, and story progress are local and cannot be recovered after deletion or device change. We recommend backing up your device regularly via iCloud.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Delete Account." This immediately and permanently erases all local game data including your gem balance, progress, unlocked items, and preferences. If you have an active Crystal Pass subscription, you must cancel it separately in your Apple ID settings.

How do tournaments work?

Tournaments are bracket-style competitions where 6-8 players compete. Entry requires an in-game gem fee. The top 3 finishers earn gems from the prize pool (1st: 50%, 2nd: 30%, 3rd: 20%). No real money is involved.

How do I challenge a friend?

After completing a game, tap the Share button on the results screen. This generates a challenge link your friend can open. They play the same board with the same pieces, and whoever scores higher wins bonus gems.

How do I stop seeing ads?

You can remove ads by subscribing to Crystal Pass (which also includes daily gems, an exclusive skin, and tournament bonuses) or by purchasing the standalone Remove Ads option if available. Rewarded ads (watch to earn gems) are always optional.

What are referral codes?

Referral codes can be redeemed once per player during onboarding or from Settings. Enter a valid code to receive bonus gems. Codes are shared by content creators and community partners.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process.
