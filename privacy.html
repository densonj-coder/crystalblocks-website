Crystal Blocks ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile puzzle game. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Data Stored On Your Device Only

The following data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress (story level, scores, streak data)
  • Gem balance and purchase history
  • Cosmetic preferences (equipped skins, backgrounds, effects)
  • Settings (sound, music, haptic preferences)
  • Daily login streak and milestone progress

This data remains entirely on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the app or use the in-app account deletion feature.

1.2 Apple Game Center

If you choose to sign in with Game Center, Apple shares your Game Center player ID with the app for leaderboard and achievement functionality. This is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. We do not store your Game Center ID on external servers.

1.3 Sign In with Apple

If you optionally sign in with your Apple ID, Apple provides a unique, app-scoped identifier. We use this solely for associating your game progress. We do not receive your real email address unless you choose to share it.

1.4 In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information, credit card numbers, or billing details. Purchase records are managed by Apple.

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements (interstitial, rewarded, and banner ads). AdMob may collect certain data as described below:

Data TypePurposeLinked to Identity?
Device Identifier (IDFA)Ad personalizationYes (if ATT granted)

You can opt out of personalized ads by declining the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt when it appears. If you decline, AdMob will serve contextual (non-personalized) ads only.

Crystal Pass subscribers and legacy Remove Ads purchasers do not see ads, and AdMob does not collect data for those users.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. App Tracking Transparency

The app requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing AdMob to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA). You can change this permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

4. Children's Privacy

Crystal Blocks is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described in Section 2 (AdMob) and as required by Apple for Game Center and App Store functionality.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" option in the app's Settings screen, which immediately clears all local data and resets your profile
  • Uninstalling the app from your device

To request deletion of any data held by third-party services (e.g., Game Center data), please contact Apple directly.

7. Security

Since game data is stored locally on your device, it benefits from the security protections built into iOS, including hardware encryption and the app sandbox. We do not operate external servers that store your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
